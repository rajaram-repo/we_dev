About SDSU Marathon


Founded in 2012, the San Diego State Uiversity Marathon is a premier race that celebrates the beauty and uniqueness of San Diego State University while raising money to help the communities that need help. As a nonprofit 501(c)(3), all net proceeds are donated to community service projects and local charitable causes. In addition, we hope to catalyze volunteerism as a way to help communities throughout our city.


VISIT SAN DIEGO


Escape the cold and run in one of the most beautiful cities in the world! San Diego boasts over 70 miles of coastline, an average daily temperature of 70.5 ºF (21 ºC) and world-class attractions such as SeaWorld, San Diego Zoo and Safari Park, and Legoland California! VOLUNTEER WITH US


FJoin the over 1,000 energetic, dedicated smiling race crew members to volunteer for event weekend on December 1, 2017! No experience is required, and optional race crew training is available. One hundred percent of our net proceeds support community service projects and local charities in San Diego. You can volunteer for as many shifts as you desire at the expo and/or on race day as long as the shifts do not overlap.
